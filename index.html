Stop Saying Hello 👋

Chat isn’t a phone call. Just ask your question. 🙃

❌ What Not to Do

✅ Do This Instead

It might feel polite to say "hi" and wait. But in chat, it's just a delay.

Typing takes longer than talking. If someone is away, they can’t respond to “hi” — but they can reply to your full question later.

Be polite, be direct:

  • ✅ "Hey! Can you review my pull request?"
  • ✅ "Hi! When’s the deadline for the doc?"
  • ✅ "Hey Jamie — mind syncing on the deck update?"
